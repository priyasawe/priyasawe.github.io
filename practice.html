The Shoulder Joint

The shoulder joint is the most mobile joint in the body. It is made up of 3 anatomical joints and 2 pseudo joints. The 3 anatomical joints include:

  1. acromioclavicuar joint
  2. glenohumeral joint
  3. costoclavicular joint
The 2 pseudo joints include:
  • scapulo-thoracic joint
  • subclavicular joint
